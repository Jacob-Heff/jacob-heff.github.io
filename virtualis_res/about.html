About Virtualis Res

Virtualis Res was started in December of 2024 by RainyFurry, and has been expanded upon by multiple prominent names in the VRChat filmmaking community. Virtualis Res takes place entirely inside of VRChat, and strictly premieres films made mostly or entirely inside of VRChat. We showcase all kinds of genres and qualities of film, from low budget horror, to mid budget drama, to high budget fantasy. There are no restrictions on your ideas, and no limitations to your creativity.
