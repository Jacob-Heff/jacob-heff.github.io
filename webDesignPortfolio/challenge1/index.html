1912 - Born

Alan Mathison Turing was born on June 23, 1912 in Maida Vale, London.

1934 - Kings College

Alan graduates with an interest in Mathematics from King's College, Cambridge.

1938 - Princeton

Alan goes to Princeton University to study mathematics and is awarded a Doctorate.

1939 - Bletchly Park

Alan is asked to join the Government Codes and Ciphers School in Bletchley Park shortly after the war was declared.

1940 - Bombe

Alan and Gordon Welchman develope the Bombe to decipher the messages sent by the Germans using the Enigma machine

1945 - OBE

At the end of the war, Alan is awarded the Excellent Order of the British Empire or OBE for short. He received this for his wartime services.
